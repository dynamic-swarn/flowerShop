Flower shops in Bhiwadi provide fresh bouquets, floral decorations, and quick delivery services for occasions like birthdays, weddings, and anniversaries. Tijara Jain Temple

Tijara Jain Temple is a famous Digambara Jain temple dedicated to Lord Chandraprabhu, the 8th Tirthankara. It was established after a sacred idol was discovered in 1956. Known for its beautiful marble architecture and peaceful atmosphere, it is considered a holy pilgrimage site and attracts devotees from across India.

Neemrana Fort Palace

Neemrana Fort Palace is a stunning 15th-century heritage fort located in Rajasthan, built in 1464 and later transformed into a luxury hotel. Nestled in the Aravalli hills, it offers breathtaking views, royal architecture, and modern amenities like pools and spa. It’s a popular weekend getaway near Delhi, blending history with comfort.
